How to get a token

Click the link next to the token-input box:



Click the "connect" button on the new page that opened and log in with your Twitch credentials:



Copy the token that is displayed on the screen, Including the "oauth:" prefix!



Connecting

After getting the token, and filling in your own Twitch username in the "your username" box,
and the channel you want to play on in the "Target channel" box, click the "save" button and then the "connect!" button
